Instructions

Use the different buttons, sliders, and checkboxes to change up the music. There are three different tracks to choose from. Both the sound and visuals can be adjusted, so have fun playing around with them. Careful when useing the color inversion checkbox, it makes the screen very flashy and hard to look at.
